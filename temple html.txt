        <h2 class="h2">The Purpose of Temples in India</h2>

        <img src="i7.jpg" class="img1">

        <p class="p1">Temples in India serve various purposes, including spiritual, religious, and cultural. They
            provide shelter, protection, and support for faithful souls. They also serve as platforms for community
            gatherings, religious rituals, and the exchange of ideas and knowledge among believers. Additionally,
            temples can serve as places for worship, learning, and religious ceremonies.</p>



        <p class="p2">Many people are relocating from their hometowns to cities in pursuit of personal and professional
            growth. Meanwhile, their families back in the villages continue to uphold the spiritual rituals. City
            dwellers occasionally return to their roots to perform prayers and maintain these traditions. However, as
            the younger generation takes over, these connections to their ancestral roots gradually fade, impacting
            their family's spiritual well-being. </p>
